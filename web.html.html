KRAFTON RULES

Official BGMI Tournament Rulebook

🎥 1. POV Recording Rules

• POV with MIC ON is compulsory for all 4 players.

• Management can ask for POV anytime.

• Keep POV safe for at least 24 Hours.

• Failure to provide POV = Direct Disqualification.

• Edited or fake POVs are not accepted.

👤 2. Account & ID Requirements

• Minimum ID Level 35+.

• Use only your own account.

• Report account issues before match starts.

• Suspicious accounts may be removed.

🚫 3. Restricted Items

• Mortar

• Emergency Pickup

• Self Revive Kit

• Fart Suit

• Penalty: -10 Points or Direct DQ.

📱 4. Device Rules

• iPads/Tablets are not allowed.

• Only legal mobile devices.

• Hacks or unauthorized tools = Permanent Ban.

🔍 5. Hack Report & Verification

• POV + Deathcam are compulsory.

• Only one proof is not accepted.

• Management decision is final.

• Discord verification may happen anytime.

🎮 6. Match Rules

• Join lobby 2–3 minutes before match.

• Result screenshots are compulsory.

• Do not leak ID & Password.

• Missed slots are your responsibility.

• Verification absence may result in DQ/Ban.

⚠️ Important Notice

Maintain Fair Play and Respect Tournament Rules.

Breaking any major rule may lead to Direct Disqualification without warning.
